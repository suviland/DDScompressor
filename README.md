**DDScompressor**\

DDS compressor for Skyrim and Fallout Auto-generate low-res texture patch for mods\
How to use?\
1.Choose your mod pathes(In 2.0 above,you can drag,manually choose&input your path.).\
2.Choose your ImageMagick path(magick.exe).(In 2.0 above,my sofware will automatically find your path through Registery).\
3.Choose your target resolution,and then choose Skip normal map or not.\
4.Click Start Compression and it will automatically generate low resolution path for your choosen mods.\
5.Refresh your modlist (MO2),and these patches will ACT AS NEW MOD PATCHES IN YOUR MOD MANAGER.Activate them and they will work correctly.\
